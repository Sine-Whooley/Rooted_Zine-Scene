About


Welcome to my Zine! I have created this space to share ideas, designs, recipes and some creativity you can acomplish in your own life. Its about slowing down and enjoying the simple things in life by making the most of what we have, and trying new things - whether you are in a tiny home, living your best van life or just your own cozy corner of the world, we hope to inspire some creativity in you.

Each issue brings together a combination of hands-on upcycling and painting projects, van and tiny home layouts, delicious recipes you can make at home or on the road. Think of it as a practical companion for anyone who wants to live lighter, stay creative, and stay connected to the world around them.
